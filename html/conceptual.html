CONCEPTUAL

 Conceptual photography is all about presenting a concept or an idea present in the mind of the photographer to other people only through the medium of photographs.
 This type of photography is generally used in advertising, where a picture helps in reiterating an idea, a tagline or a catchphrase for a product or service.

"Life is a story. Make yours the best seller"

"Silence is the most powerful scream."

"We could never learn to be brave and patient, if there were only joy in the world."

"Life is the greatest journey you will be ever on."

"adness flies away on the wings of time"

"Life is short. Smile while you still have teeth!"

"Everybody laughs in the same language."
